How to embed?

Microsoft Office Web Apps

You must share you documents, before you can embed them. To share you documents:

  1. Log in to your WindowsLive account at Live.com
  2. Click Office and than Your Documents at the top menu
  3. Drag the mouse over the document that you wish to share
  4. Click Share and than Edit permissions
  5. Set document access to Everyone (public)
  6. Click Save button - your document will be listed at Shared by me section

Documents and Spreadsheets

  1. Drag the mouse over the document that you wish to embed
  2. Click Share and than Embed
  3. Copy the embed code from texarea
  4. Paste it to the WindowsLive blocktype

Presentations, Notebooks and Folders

  1. Drag the mouse over the document (or folder) that you wish to embed
  2. Click More than Share and than Embed
  3. Copy the embed code from texarea
  4. Paste it to the WindowsLive blocktype

Hotmail Calendar

  1. Log in to your WindowsLive account at Live.com
  2. Click Hotmail and than Calendar at the top menu
  3. Click Share link on the left upper side
  4. Click the name of the calendar that you wish to embed
  5. On Sharing settings page, click Put on your website link
  6. Customize calendar apperance and than copy embed code from textarea
  7. Paste it to the WindowsLive blocktype
Note: You can embed large or small calendar. Small calendar is limited to fixed set of values for width and height. You cannot use values other than that.

Bing Maps

  1. Open Bing Maps
  2. Find the map that you wish to embed
  3. Click on Share your map button (letter icon on right bottom side)
  4. Customize the apperance of the map and copy embed code
  5. Paste it to the WindowsLive blocktype
