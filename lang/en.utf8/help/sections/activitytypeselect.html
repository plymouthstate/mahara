Activity Type

Select from this drop down menu the notification type you wish to view.

New forum post:notification of a new post to a forum topic you have subscribed to

Feedback: notification of any feedback or comments received on any views, artefacts or blogs within your ePortfolio.

Group message: notification sent to group owners when new views are submitted to a group

System message: automatically generated by the system or sent to you by one of the site administrators.

Message from other users: sent to you directly from other users of the site.

New view access: notification you or one of your groups have been given access to a new or existing non-public view.

Watchlist: notification of activity on any artefact, view, group or blog you are monitoring.
