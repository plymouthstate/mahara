Notifications

You may select how you receive notifications for each activity type.

Notification Types

Activity Log: your notifications area will display a list of activity notifications received.

Email: your primary email address will receive an email each time the activity occurs.

Email digest: your primary email address will receive an email each day with a list of the last 24 hoursâ€™ activities

Note: If you select either email option all activities will also be recorded in your notifications log as they happen. They will all be marked as already read. These notifications automatically expire and are removed from your log after 60 days.

Activity Types

System messages: automatically generated by the system or sent to you by one of the site administrators.

Messages from other users: sent to you directly from other users of the site.

Feedback: notification of any feedback or comments received on any views, artefacts or blogs within your ePortfolio.

Watchlist: notification of activity on any artefact, view, group or blog you are monitoring.

New views access: notification you or one of your groups have been given access to a new or existing non-public view.
